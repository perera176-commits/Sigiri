About WEBP Format

WEBP is a modern image format developed by Google that provides superior lossless and lossy compression for images on the web. WEBP files are 25-35% smaller than JPEG at equivalent quality and support transparency like PNG. The format also supports animation, making it a versatile replacement for JPEG, PNG, and GIF. While browser support has improved significantly, some older systems may require conversion to more compatible formats.
