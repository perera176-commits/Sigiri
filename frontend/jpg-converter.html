About JPG Format

JPG/JPEG (Joint Photographic Experts Group) is the most widely used image format for photographs and web images. It uses lossy compression to significantly reduce file size while maintaining acceptable quality, making it perfect for photos, web graphics, and digital photography. JPG files are universally compatible across all devices, platforms, and software, but don't support transparency or animations.
