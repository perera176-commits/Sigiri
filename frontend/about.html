Our Mission

At SIGIRI, we believe that file conversion should be simple, fast, and accessible to everyone. Our mission is to provide intelligent, AI-powered tools that make it effortless to convert files between different formats while maintaining the highest quality and security standards.

We're committed to removing the barriers that prevent people from working with their files the way they want. Whether you're a professional designer, a student, a content creator, or just someone who needs to convert a file occasionally, SIGIRI is here to help.

Our Story

SIGIRI was founded in 2025 with a simple goal: make file conversion accessible and hassle-free for everyone. Frustrated by complicated software and unreliable online tools, our team of engineers and designers set out to create something better.

We combined cutting-edge AI technology with intuitive design to build a platform that's both powerful and easy to use. Today, SIGIRI serves users in over 150 countries, processing millions of files every month.

Our Name: SIGIRI is inspired by the ancient Sigiriya rock fortress in Sri Lanka, a marvel of engineering and innovation. Like our namesake, we strive to build something remarkable that stands the test of time.
